Background

I am a student of Carnegie Mellon's Masters of HCI program. I graduated from Cornell University and spent much of my undergraduate career doing academic research with the Interaction Design Lab. I worked on projects that explored the use of colored light as an ambient display of mood and living plants as information displays. My future goal is to be able to adapt and design for any situation, whether if it involves physical devices or is an intangible service. Ultimately, I wish to design compelling experiences that play with the intersection between the physical and virtual worlds.
